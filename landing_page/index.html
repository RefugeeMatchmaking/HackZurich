We connect refugees with locals

Refugee Lighthouse connects locals with refugees for simple social interactions. This could be an afternoon coffee, walk in the park, a visit to a museum or whatever your saturday afternoon brings you.

Test it now

HackZurich project to help refugees integrate

We worked on this project during the HackZurich15 hackathon on the 2-4 October in Zurich. Everything you see here is open source - feel free to clone it, fork it, or send us pull requests!

Connecting Communities

Apply online

Apply as a local or as a refugee in just a matter of minutes, and get a match from an algorith designed to keep you safe.

Meet for coffee

Have a chat with some other members of your community and those who are just arriving. See how you can help each other.

Build relationships

It's not with community centers that you will rebuild communities. It's with face-to-face communication and friendship.

Spread the love

Everyone deserves to be treated with dignity and respect. Some of us fell into an unfortunate situation, let's help them out!
